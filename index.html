About PosterBay MakerHub

PosterBay started as a premium, design-forward poster brand in Bengaluru, dedicated to customizing creators' personal spaces. As our community of makers, builders, and designers grew, we noticed a major gap in access to rapid, high-quality 3D printing and custom hardware prototyping.

Today, we’re expanding PosterBay into the MakerHub—combining our passion for premium wall art and custom displays with on-demand 3D printing and small-batch fabrication services.
